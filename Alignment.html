Intro

A wheel alignment is deinfed as the adjustment of the suspension and steering to ensure proper veicle handling with minimum tire wear. A new vehicle has its alignment set by the factory.
After being on the raod and accumulating milage the alignment can change. The change in the alignment angles can affect the vehicle in a few ways.

  1. Damaged or broken steering and suspension components
  2. Cause the driver to feel pulling or shaking while driving the vehicle
  3. excessive wear of the tires
